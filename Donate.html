Donate

Fueling Futures: Donate to Empower Foster Youth in Diesel Tech Education!

By contributing to our cause, you play a vital role in breaking down barriers and creating lasting impact. With your support, foster youth gain access to a specialized vocational program that not only imparts valuable diesel tech skills but also instills confidence and resilience.

Your generosity helps build a foundation for these young individuals, paving the way for self-sufficiency and success in their chosen careers. Every donation, no matter the size, makes a meaningful difference in the lives of foster youth pursuing a brighter, empowered future.

Together, let's transform lives and ignite opportunities for success in the world of diesel technology.

The Bridge to Independence is a 501(c)3 tax-exempt organization, and your donation is tax-deductible within the guidelines of U.S. law. To claim a donation as a deduction on your U.S. taxes, please keep your payment confirmation as your official record.

Support our cause by contributing through Zelle, Cash App, or by check. Your donation helps us make a positive impact, providing vital resources and support to those in need. Join us in making a difference today

Donating via Cash App or Zelle

If you want to donate through Zelle or Cash App you can scan the respective QR Codes or click their buttons.

Donating via Check

    If you would like to donate via a check, follow these steps:
  1. Make the check payable to The Bridge to Independence
  2. Mail the check to our Headquarters: 1201 N Orange Street #400 Wilmington, DE 19801
  3. Keep a record of this charitable donation for your taxes
