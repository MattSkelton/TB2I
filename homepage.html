Our Mission

It is the mission, duty, and sole purpose of The Bridge to Independence is to provide two-year comprehensive vocational and life skills education in college campus environment to at-risk young adults between the ages 18 to 25, with the emphasis placed on young adults who are aging out of the foster care system. This “Bridge” will provide all the necessary support to each student as they travel through the difficult transition from dependence to independence.

Who We Serve

Empowering Foster Youth Transitioning to Adulthood
through Trucking Education

At The Bridge to Independence, we specialize in empowering foster youth transitioning to adulthood through vocational trucking education. Our targeted programs provide accessible and affordable opportunities, equipping these young individuals with practical skills and personal development for a promising future in the trucking industry. With a commitment to breaking the cycle of uncertainty, we focus on fostering independence and success, ensuring a confident start to their adult lives. At The Bridge to Independence, we believe in providing the tools for not only a fulfilling career but also a self-assured journey into the next chapter of life.

Our Vision

Paving Roads to Independence and Success

At The Bridge to Independence, our vision is to be a guiding force in transforming the lives of vulnerable youth, particularly those aging out of the foster care system. We aspire to create a future where every individual, regardless of their past challenges, has the opportunity to achieve independence and success. Through our vocational trucking education, we envision empowering these young individuals with the skills, confidence, and resilience needed to navigate the road ahead. Our commitment extends beyond the classroom, aiming to instill a sense of purpose and self-reliance that transcends the trucking industry. By envisioning a world where our graduates thrive personally and professionally, we seek to build a community of empowered individuals who drive change, break barriers, and inspire others to follow a path of lifelong learning and achievement.
