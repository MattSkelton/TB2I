Vocational Skills

Rev Up Your Career: Diesel Vocational Training at Its Best!

The Journey

Embark on a transformative journey at The Bridge to Independence's 2-year diesel tech program, where hands-on training hones your skills in engine diagnostics and repair. Our comprehensive curriculum ensures a mastery of the intricacies of diesel mechanics, setting the stage for a successful career in the field.

Program Description

Prepare for a rewarding career as an entry-level truck/heavy equipment technician through The Bridge's 2-year diesel tech program. Our curriculum is meticulously crafted to emphasize hands-on learning, utilizing actual customer projects and mock-up units, mirroring real industry scenarios. Dive into extensive shop work covering engines, transmissions, differentials, brakes, steering, suspension, cooling, hydraulics, undercarriages, fuel and air systems, and receive an introduction to vehicle/equipment operation.

Throughout the program, theory and troubleshooting are integrated, addressing challenges in the repair and maintenance of diverse automotive components. Safety and basic welding procedures are also incorporated into the coursework, providing a holistic skill set for our graduates.

Successful completion of each semester, or instructor permission, is essential for progression, ensuring that students grasp the foundational knowledge required for the subsequent phases of the program. Placement in specific English and math courses is determined by the college assessment test, ensuring a tailored educational experience for each student. Join us in transforming your passion for diesel technology into a thriving career.
